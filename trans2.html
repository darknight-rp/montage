Silos and Storage Tanks

🏗️ Installation of Industrial Silos and Storage Systems 📌 Service Overview The installation of industrial silos and storage systems includes construction, assembly, and commissioning of facilities for storing, transporting, and processing bulk materials such as grain, cement, feed, powders, and more. These systems are essential for: - Agriculture - Food industry - Construction - Heavy industry 🏢 What the installation includes 1. Site preparation - Construction of foundations (concrete base) - Leveling and positioning - Preparation for heavy equipment ➡️ Silos are installed on reinforced concrete bases or metal structures 2. Silo assembly - Installation of cylindrical panels (bolted or welded) - Roof and conical bottom installation - Sealing and protection ➡️ Silos are typically modular structures assembled on-site 3. Equipment installation - Screw conveyors and transport systems - Elevators and chain conveyors - Filters, ventilation, and dust extraction - Monitoring systems (temperature, level) ➡️ Complete transport and dosing systems are often integrated 4. Storage systems In addition to silos, we also build: - Racking systems - Automated warehouses - Logistics solutions ➡️ Storage systems enable efficient material and goods management 5. Testing and commissioning - Stability checks - Testing of all mechanisms - Staff training ⚙️ Types of silos - Grain silos - Cement silos - Powder material silos - Wood and waste silos ➡️ Used for storing grain, cement, ash, pellets, and other materials 🚀 Advantages - High efficiency and automation - Reduced material loss - Safe storage - Optimized logistics - Long service life 🏗️ Suitable for - Grain bases - Factories and plants - Ports and terminals - Logistics centers - Cement and concrete facilities
